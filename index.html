About Form Theory

We start by looking at how your spine and joints are moving, what muscles are tight or overworking, and what’s actually causing your discomfort. Then we use focused chiropractic adjustments and supportive hands-on treatment to relieve tension, restore motion, and help your body function the way it should.

Led by Dr. Logan Donaldson, Form Theory provides evidence-based chiropractic care to reduce pain, improve mobility, and help you get back to feeling like yourself again.

If you want a chiropractor who listens, explains what’s going on, and gives you the care your body needs to feel better, you’re in the right place.
